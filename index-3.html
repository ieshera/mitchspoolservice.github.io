Main Services

Openings

We are intimately familiar with all types of pools, whether it be in-ground concrete, vinyl or fiberglass as well as above-ground pools. We recommend opening your pool in late March or early April to avoid water chemistry imbalances. If you choose to open later, we recommend shocking the pool in late March or early April to help stabilize the water.

Seasonal Maintenance

We customize our service and chemical packages to the needs of your pool. Along with offering Weekly, Bi-Weekly, and Monthly services, we also offer vacation vistis. Vacation vistis allow us to take care of your pool while you are on vacation or out of town on business. We will maintain your pool just like we do our Weekly Maintenance Service pools, so you don't have to worry about your pool while you are away.
Another essential service we offer are filter cleanings. Every year or two it is recommended to have your filter assembly disassembled and chemically cleaned in order to restore it to like-new condition.

Closings

After a season of swimming fun, closing your pool properly will make opening it much easier next season. Covering the pool is an economical and easy way to care for the pool in the off-season. The pool cover keeps debris and leaves out of the pool, reduces chemical and electrical expenses by at least half, and reduces maintenance required when opening the next season.



Service

Leak Detection is another service offered to Mitch's clients. Underground leaks can cost you more than just water loss. You could experience deck shifting, liner float, ground erosion, or pump damage. Our experienced Technicians will pinpoint the problem using the latest techniques and equipment.

Mitch offers customizable service ot meet the needs of your pool. If there are any services you require that are not already listed, feel free to contact Mitch to talk about it.

Rennovations

Our pool renovation can range from a small repair to complete facelift. This includes equipment, tile, coping, plaster, decks, rubber joint, and anything else that your pool may need.

When your existing swimming pool needs more than basic service or repair, let us give it a total makeover! Mitch's Pool Service will deliver more than you ever imagined possible with a complete modernization and style update.
